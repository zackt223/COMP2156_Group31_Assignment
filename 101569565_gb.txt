George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
It was founded in 1967 and is named after George Brown, a Father of Confederation.
The college serves over 30,000 full-time students across multiple campuses.

George Brown offers programs in Business, Technology, Health Sciences,
Arts, Community Services, and Hospitality.
Programs range from certificates and diplomas to bachelor degrees.

COMP2156 - DevOps for System Administration teaches students
how to bridge the gap between software development and IT operations.
The course focuses on automation, collaboration, and continuous delivery.
