George Brown College is a publicly funded college of applied arts and technology
located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Since its founding in 1967, the college
has been dedicated to providing students with career-ready education and training
that meets the evolving demands of the modern workforce.

George Brown College serves a diverse student body of over 30,000 full-time learners.
The college offers certificates, diplomas, postgraduate, and degree-level programs.
Its strong industry connections ensure that program content stays current and relevant,
and many students secure employment with their co-op employers upon graduation.
